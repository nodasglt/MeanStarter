Dr. Cleopatra Bardaki

Dr Cleopatra Bardaki holds a PhD in Information Systems and an MSc in Information Systems from Athens University of Economics and Business (AUEB) and a BSc (with honors) in Informatics and Telecommunications from University of Athens. Her doctoral research has been funded by a 4-year scholarship from Bodossaki Foundation and she has received more academic distinctions and scholarships during her studies. Cleopatra has published over thirty papers in academic journals, proceedings of international academic conferences and edited books. She is a member of SCORE (Supply Chain and Demand Management, Collaboration and Electronic Services) group of ELTRUN Research Center of AUEB since 2005. She has participated as lead researcher in leading edge research & development projects funded mostly from the EU. Currently, Cleopatra is a lead researcher in two H2020 projects and one FP7 projects, having also project management responsibilities. Moreover, she supports three PhD candidates in their research.
